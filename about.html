The Beginning

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of hardware enthusiasts, AURA started with a simple mission: to make premium, high-performance electronics accessible to everyone. What began as a small boutique PC building service in a garage has rapidly evolved into a leading online destination for all consumer electronics.

We realized that the tech retail experience was broken—filled with confusing jargon, poor customer support, and uninspired curation. We set out to change that by focusing on rigorous testing, expert curation, and a frictionless shopping experience.
